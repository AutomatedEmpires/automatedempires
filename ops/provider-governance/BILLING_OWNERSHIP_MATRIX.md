# Billing Ownership Matrix

**Implementation snapshot:** 2026-07-12
**Authority:** Canonical non-secret billing and cost-ownership register derived from the Agent 3 account-governance reports
**Operating mode:** Documentation only; no purchase, plan, cap, renewal, payment, provider, or production state was changed

## Interpretation

This matrix separates the provider account owner from the billing owner. A sole technical owner, a payment method, a project name, or access to a mailbox does not establish who is accountable for invoices or authorized to approve spend.

Status vocabulary is limited to **known**, **unknown**, **inferred**, **blocked**, and **needs founder**. Historical amounts are identified as historical and must not be booked as current spend. Unknown and blocked costs are not zero. Provider free allowances, notification thresholds, prepaid balances, or feature-disable switches are not treated as hard spend caps unless the provider evidence establishes that behavior.

## Monthly ownership control

The billing owner and backup for each retained provider should maintain a non-secret monthly record containing:

1. plan/base charge, billed seats, usage, credits, projected overage, taxes/fees, invoice state, and next renewal;
2. provider-level controls and their actual semantics, including whether each is an alert, prepaid balance, feature switch, pause, soft limit, or hard stop;
3. venture/resource allocation, shared parent overhead, and an explicit unallocated bucket;
4. changes approved during the month and the founder decision reference for purchases, cap increases, legal/payment activation, or production pauses;
5. orphan/duplicate candidates held for review, with no cancellation or deletion authority implied;
6. the billing owner, backup, escalation path, and evidence date, recorded as roles or approved business identities rather than private contacts.
