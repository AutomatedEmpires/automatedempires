**Prepared:** 2026-07-12

## How to read this map

- **known** means the resource or boundary is established by the current evidence or identified historical evidence described in the cell.
- **unknown** means the evidence does not establish the resource or posture.
- **inferred** means names or configuration indicate a likely mapping, but the controlling account, owner, or live use is not proven.
- **blocked** means current provider access did not permit verification.
- **needs founder** means business ownership, retention, activation, or transfer intent requires an explicit founder decision.

**Evidence provenance:** `recorded` means the fact came from the sanitized portfolio evidence and was not freshly reverified at every provider. It may appear in cell prose, but it is not a status. Every mapped cell below leads with exactly one canonical status.

A venture-named repository, project, application, token, folder, or provider configuration is a useful operational boundary, but **project separation is not independent ownership**. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, the resources below remain controlled by a parent/shared account with unverified or shared billing, recovery, administrator, and transfer authority. No venture or provider is represented as transfer-ready or rollout-ready by this map.

## Ownership conclusions

1. The parent/shared control planes remain GitHub, Vercel, GoDaddy/DNS, Doppler, Supabase, Clerk, PostHog, Sentry, Cloudinary, Mapbox, and most mail administration. Provider subresources do not make a venture independently owned.
2. Explore&Earn and Sweepza have the strongest recorded Stripe account separation, and Explore&Earn has a venture-named Resend team/domain, but legal ownership, backup administration, recovery, billing authority, and recipient acceptance are still unproven.
3. PinnedAtlas is materially active and must be classified immediately. Komfort Killz and Just Jesus Bro are unclassified; the default treatment is parent-held reservation only until the founder records a different classification.
4. No venture in this map satisfies the complete transfer test: verified legal/account and billing owners, two controlled admin/recovery paths with MFA, least-privilege access, current cost/renewal evidence, a complete dependency inventory, recipient acceptance, and tested continuity/rollback.

## Safe maintenance rule

Update this map only from non-secret provider exports or approved founder decisions. Record roles and business ownership categories rather than private identities, recovery destinations, provider object identifiers, or credential values. Any invite, role change, billing change, domain action, DNS edit, credential operation, resource transfer, activation, or deletion requires separate approval and execution evidence.
