## Governance rules

- Asset groups organize access; they are not proof of legal ownership or transfer readiness.
- One venture's Page and Instagram profile belong in its matching group only.
- Parent operators may retain portfolio administration, but venture-level access should remain least-privileged and documented.
- Never use `AE | Archive / Legacy` as a deletion queue. Moving an asset there requires explicit founder approval and a recorded reason.
- Do not move an asset between portfolios or transfer ownership as part of group cleanup.
- Do not attach an ad account to any group in this workstream. This setup does not authorize advertising or boosted posts.
- ORAN must remain free of Meta Pixel/dataset installation under this mission's rules.
- Sweepza must remain business-facing giveaway campaign infrastructure in development. Use only conservative Page categories such as Business service, Software, Marketing service, or Advertising/Marketing; never use gambling, casino, lottery, or betting categories or terminology.
- Sweepza's default campaign status is `legal_review_required`. Only a separate legal and founder approval may set `approved_for_public`; group creation or Page setup is not campaign approval.
- Never attach a Sweepza ad account, Pixel, dataset, conversion, catalog, commerce/payment asset, entrant-data flow, or public campaign here. Do not scrape or repost third-party giveaways, and stop if Meta flags the Page or category for review.
- A proposed handle, source-code URL, or public Page name is not sufficient ownership evidence; verify it in Business Settings.
