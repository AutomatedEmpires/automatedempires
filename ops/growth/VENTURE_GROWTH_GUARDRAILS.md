# Venture Growth and Positioning Guardrails

**Effective:** 2026-07-12
**Authority:** `docs/VENTURE_OPERATING_DEFINITIONS.md`
**Mode:** Messaging and planning only; this file authorizes no campaign, spend, tracking, partnership claim, outreach, or launch.

Growth work must describe the product that is actually authorized, distinguish current availability from future direction, and avoid claims that expand legal or operating responsibility.

| Venture | Positioning to use | Claims and actions to avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Explore&Earn | Seeker-first seasonal/work-travel discovery with clear housing, meals, pay, trust, and map/list/swipe exploration; visual overhaul must be visionary and grounded in the actual repo/routes/components/data | New categories, replacement navigation, safety guarantees, repo-detached redesign, or mechanics churn without evidence and a dated decision |
| Sweepza | Modern discovery of third-party sweepstakes/giveaways from official sources plus host/sponsor/partner submissions, with verification state and official link-outs | Saying Sweepza runs promotions, sets rules, determines eligibility, processes entries, selects winners, processes promotion payments or prize funds, guarantees legality/odds, or has an unverified partnership |
| LogLoads | Forestry/logistics coordination software | Calling it a broker, carrier, payment processor, or dispatch-for-hire operator; promising production readiness from a Preview or limiter adapter |
| Lake & Pine | Intelligent cleaning/local-service booking, planning, property preference, recurring-service, job-status, communication, and operator tooling | Reducing it to a brochure site; claiming payments or capacity-confirmed booking before those phases are approved and proven |
| ORAN | Chat-first guided intake that understands need/context and routes people to verified, eligibility/location-relevant providers in an initial protected North Idaho/Eastern Washington MVP | Generic resource dumps, broad irrelevant directories, user/behavioral tracking or pixels, medical/legal/political/emergency advice, implied partnerships, unsupported matches, or public-launch claims before gates pass. Privacy-scrubbed operational health logs are safety-only, never profiling/attribution/ads/product analytics |
| BidSpace | Host–vendor marketplace for temporary commercial/vendor spaces, with profiles, space listings/maps, bidding/application, communication, outreach, verification, and trust | Generic-auction or physical-goods framing, real/public bidding or booking before acceptance gates pass, live-payment/Stripe Connect claims, unsupported foot-traffic/terms, or claims that a demo/deployment is a live marketplace |
| AutomatedEmpires | Parent portfolio command layer for status, providers, rollout, governance, cost/risk, orchestration, and transfer readiness | Generic AI-agency positioning, invented performance claims, public exposure of private operational data, or treating child-venture provider access as a shared product feature |

## Sweepza listing-copy minimums

Every listing design or content plan must provide space for the official source, official rules link, official entry link, verification status, expiry, and a clear statement that the promotion is run by the identified sponsor/host unless a later approved exception says otherwise. Do not copy long source descriptions, rules, or images without rights.

## Launch-language rule

Use precise states such as `concept`, `active build`, `protected preview`, `controlled candidate`, or `publicly available` only when evidence supports them. A domain, provider project, production-class deployment, or build result alone is not proof that a venture or feature is live.
